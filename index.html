About

With a screen which is constantly scrolling, student have to have both proper focus and English skills to keep on with the flow of words.

  • Different levels of text with different speeds
  • Officially verified by a UC Admissions officer and AP English teacher with more than twenty years of experience
  • Optimized for reading - best color contrasts between the background and text

Levels we offer

To cater to all the students's ages, levels and needs, we have implemented three levels of reading difficulty to help get a better idea of where they stand with regard to the rest of their peers.

Level One

This is the most basic level. It consists of simple English words and structures. It is recommended for kids aged 3-6.

Level One

Levels Two

This is the second level. It consists of slightly harder English words and structures. It is recommended for kids aged 5-9 with advance reading skills.

Level Two

Level Three

This is the level targetted towards middle school children. It consists of a bit complex English words and structures. It is recommended for kids aged 9-13.
